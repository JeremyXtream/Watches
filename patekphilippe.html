Patek Philippe:

Patek Philippe, founded by Antoni Patek and Adrien Philippe is a Swiss luxury watch and clock manufacturer founded in 1839, located in Canton of Geneva. It designs and manufactures timepieces as well as movements, including some of the most complicated mechanical watches.

Lets have a look at some of this brands exquisite watches!

The Nautilus has epitomized the elegant sports watch since 1976. Forty years later, it comprises a splendid collection of models for men and women. The picture features the Nautilus 5908 I consider to be very classy and elegant.
This watch belongs to the family of 'Complications'. They call these watches a complication because the creation of a self-winding watch that displays more than just the time and date requires a lot of skill and precision. This particular model, the 5147g Complications, features an annual calendar and displays the moon phases.
The Grandmaster Chime is the most complicated Patek Philippe wristwatch ever made. It consists of twenty complications, a reversible case and two independent dials and six patented innovations. The development, production and assembly process covered a staggering 100,000 hours.
