Jacob&Co:

Jacob & Co., sometimes referred to as "Jacob And Co" or "Jacob And Company," is founded in 1986 by diamond designer Jacob Arabo. The watches of this brand is the most expensive among most watch brands. This company uses a movement called Tourbillion which is nowadays found only in the most luxurious watches.

The Jacob & Co. Astronomia Casino, also known as Jacob & Co. Astronomia Gambler, is another unique creation from Jacob & Co. These watches are made with the intention of being over-engineered, fun and ultra high end, which is very clearly seen in this watch!
The 'Billionaire' is a 1 of 1 watch which consists of 260 carats of baguette-cut diamonds. This is skeletonized watch and of course has a tourbillion movement. The diamonds are cased in a 18 carat white gold structure. This watch is valued at 18 million USD and was bought by retired boxing champion Floyd Mayweather.
